Google Maps

I am currently working as an Associate Product Manager at Google. I oversee product development and design for Google Maps, with a focus on Local Factual User Generated Content.

In the summer of 2015, I served as an Associate Product Manager Intern at Google, also on the Maps team with a focus on Local Actions.

My responsibilities included working with designers and developers to develop features to allow users to personalize their Google Maps experience, as well as researching ways to increase the number of users who made use of personalized features on Google Maps for Mobile devices.

Google Politics and Elections

In the summer of 2014, I worked as a Software Engineering Intern for Google's Politics and Elections team, where my responsibilites included adding new features to the Voter Information Tool for making election information more accessible.

I also participated in the 2014 Google Social Impact Hackathon, where my team received second place for an application allowing users to recieve text alerts when a natural disaster was predicted for their area.

The Onion

I am a Features contributor for The Onion, where I submit biweekly joke packets for Infographic assignments.
